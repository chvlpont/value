Our Story

In the vibrant Italian landscape of 1980, I embarked on a thrilling adventure, laying the foundation for my clothing brand, "Value." Italy's breathtaking scenery and rich culture became my wellspring of inspiration. My designs were born from the essence of Italy's diverse regions, with their unique styles and heritage.

This journey was no cakewalk. I encountered numerous challenges along the way. Yet, I persevered, traveling from the northern Alps to the sunny southern coasts to source fine fabrics and craft distinctive designs.

As the brand gained momentum, people began to embrace "Value" for its Italian-influenced flair. This adventure, marked by ups and downs, continued to evolve, leading to new inspirations and fashion creations. Today, "Value" stands as a testament to an Italian-themed fashion odyssey, celebrating the enduring allure of Italy.
